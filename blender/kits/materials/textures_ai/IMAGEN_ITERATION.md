## Iteration Rules

- Keep all Imagen outputs in this folder as candidates until human review.
- Do not promote candidates into `blender/kits/materials/textures/` until they beat the deterministic texture for the same surface.
- Do not add new material names for these textures; the shared material library is closed.
- Validate candidate files for image shape, seam quality, palette fit, and pixel-art readability before review.

## Review Checklist

- The image is a flat material texture, not a building/object/scene render.
- The texture reads as the requested surface at isometric game-camera distance.
- The palette stays near the transcribed albedo ramps in `docs/ART_REFERENCE_ABBEY.md`.
- Edges can tile without obvious seams.
- There are no labels, watermarks, borders, UI fragments, or checkerboard backgrounds.
- Shading is local material detail only, with no baked scene lighting or cast shadow.
