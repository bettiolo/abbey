# P3-09 ‚Äî Laws and decrees

## What changed

Standing **laws**: five policy groups the player legislates, each with exactly one active
option ‚Äî **Food** (Equal / Workers First / Beast Share / Fasting), **Night labour** (No Work
After Bell / Paid Risk / Forced), **Burial** (Full Rites / Mass Graves / Use the Dead),
**Hound** (Family / Weapon / Chained / Sacred) and **Old rites** (Tolerate Offerings / Forbid
Pagan Rites). A new **`LawSystem`** (on `WorldSystems`, singleton, `[ExecuteAlways]`) holds the
active option per group; changing one is a cooldown-gated **decree** (event-logged). Every
option carries a data-driven mechanical effect:

- **Food** ‚Üí the dawn **ration pass** (`IssueRations`) draws Food from the `ResourceLedger`
  per the active table (workers vs idle share, hound keep, Fasting hunger + sanity pressure).
- **Night labour** ‚Üí gates the P3-08 overdrive levers through
  `OverdriveSystem.PermissionProvider` (`IsPermittedByLaw`).
- **Burial** ‚Üí `ProcessBurial` pays each option's costs/refunds and stamps a **grave tag** when
  a villager dies (deaths are scanned from the event log at dawn).
- **Hound** ‚Üí writes `HoundEvolutionSystem.Doctrine` (consumed by P3-07 evolution).
- **Old rites** ‚Üí daily sanctity / old-faith pressure + offering / secret-rite tags.

Every option also writes a durable **tag** (`LawTags`, e.g. `mass_graves_active`,
`forced_labour_night`, `pagan_rites_forbidden`, per-death `grave_used`) that P3-10 moral
pressures and P3-11 consequence nightmares read. All balance lives in **`LawsConfig`**
(Resources path `LawsConfig`, coded defaults ‚Äî no asset required). Deterministic (no RNG).

Added: `Runtime/Decrees/LawSystem.cs`, `Runtime/Decrees/LawDefinitions.cs`,
`Runtime/Decrees/LawsConfig.cs`, `Runtime/Debug/LawsDebugPanel.cs`,
`Tests/EditMode/LawEffectsTests.cs`. Modified: `Economy/ResourceLedger.cs` (adds the
`IssueRation` rationing draw), `Editor/PrototypeSceneBuilder.cs` (spawns `LawSystem` +
`LawsDebugPanel`). The Hound-doctrine input and the overdrive permission gate were already
exposed by P3-07 / P3-08, so `HoundEvolutionSystem` / `OverdriveSystem` need no code change ‚Äî
`LawSystem` writes `Doctrine` and sets `PermissionProvider`. Debug surface: **`LawsDebugPanel`**
(key **L** ‚Äî the F1-F12 row is fully allocated).

## Visual verification

1. `Tools ‚Üí Abbey ‚Üí Build Prototype Scene`, then press Play. `WorldSystems` now also holds a
   `LawSystem`; `DebugPanels` holds a `LawsDebugPanel`.
2. Press **L** to open the **Laws** panel (left of the overdrive stack). It lists each group's
   active option with its standing tag (defaults: Equal / Paid Risk / Full Rites / Family /
   Forbid Pagan Rites), the decree cooldown, today's ration math, the night-labour gate state,
   and the accrued P3-10 pressures. While the panel is open, **Shift+1..5** decrees the next
   option for Food / Night labour / Burial / Hound / Old rites.
3. **Fasting.** Shift+1 until Food shows **Fasting**. **Correct:** the panel's ration line
   shows the cut worker/idle amounts and a "fasting: ‚àísanity, +hunger each" line; each dawn the
   `RationsIssued` log draws less Food, the **EconomyDebugPanel** (F2) food drain slows, and the
   **SanityDebugPanel** (F9) bars drift down. **Broken:** rations unchanged, or food drains as
   fast as under Equal, or sanity is untouched.
4. **Mass Graves + a death.** Shift+3 until Burial shows **Mass Graves**, then kill a villager
   (let a monster reach one, or force it). At the next dawn the burial is processed:
   **correct** = a `burial ‚Ä¶ tag=grave_mass` record in the event log, the panel's fear/sanctity
   pressures move, and no candles are spent (contrast Full Rites, which debits candles and lifts
   mercy). Flip to **Use the Dead** and a later death refunds meat/herbs into the ledger and
   books nightmare pressure with a `grave_used` tag.
5. **Night labour gate.** Shift+2 until Night labour shows **No Work After Bell**, then open the
   **Overdrive** panel (F12): Candle Line / Forced Night Work / Volunteer Watch now show
   `[barred]` and refuse to fire (`overdrive_refused reason=not_permitted` in the log). Flip to
   **Paid Risk** or **Forced** and they fire again. **Broken:** levers fire under No Work After
   Bell, or a non-night-labour lever (Bell Toll) gets barred.
6. **Hound doctrine.** Shift+4 to change the Hound law; open the hound / beast overlay and
   confirm the doctrine the P3-07 evolution system reads flips to match (Family / Weapon /
   Chained / Sacred), biasing the path scores.
7. Cross-check balance against data, not code: open `LawsConfig` (or the coded defaults in
   `Runtime/Decrees/LawsConfig.cs`) and confirm the ration tables, night-labour permission
   matrix, burial costs/refunds/pressures, old-rite daily deltas and the decree cooldown match
   what the L panel and the logs show.
