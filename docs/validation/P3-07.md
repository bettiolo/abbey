# P3-07 — Hound evolution paths and beast status

## What changed

The long-arc **evolution** layer on top of the P2-05 bond: how the settlement *treats*
the Black Hound across the year hardens it into one of five paths — **Guardian / War /
Starved / Sacred / Broken** — that change its night behaviour and expose a **beast
status** (-1 feared … +1 beloved) for the P3-10 pressures and the P3-14 end summary. A
new `HoundEvolutionSystem` (on `WorldSystems`) evaluates once per **dawn**: it reads the
`HoundController`'s accumulated treatment counters (feed events, allied fights, solo
hunts, rites, chain-time, injuries) + bond averages + a **`HoundDoctrine`** input
(written by the P3-09 Hound law; `Neutral` default), scores the five paths through the
**`HoundEvolutionConfig`** ScriptableObject (Resources path `HoundEvolutionConfig`, coded
defaults — no asset required), adopts the dominant path over the adopt threshold and
**locks** it past the lock threshold. Adopting a path pushes its behaviour parameters
(aggression, bell response, villager comfort, hunt-alone / abbey-only / unreliable flags)
onto the controller. Every transition is event-logged (`hound_evolved`, from→to, plus a
`locked` record). All balance is data (per-path score weights, thresholds, behaviour sets,
beast-status values) in `HoundEvolutionConfig` — nothing in the MonoBehaviours. The beast
stays **exempt** from sanity (no record — it is not a `VillagerAgent`) and from light-band
combat penalties (`LightBandCombatResolver` beast-exempt) on **every** path.

Debug surface: the **F1 DebugOverlay** "Black Hound" section now shows the treatment
counters, the current `PATH` (+ `LOCKED`), the doctrine, the `beastStatus`, and the five
per-path score values with the dominant score.

New test fixtures for this task:

- EditMode: `Abbey.Tests.EditMode.HoundEvolutionTests` — **12 tests**. Scripted treatment
  histories drive each of the five paths (`HoundTreatmentSample` → `HoundEvolutionConfig`):
  Guardian (feed + co-fight + trust), War (constant combat + Weapon doctrine), Starved
  (neglect + hunger + solo hunts), Sacred (rites + Sacred doctrine), Broken (chain + pain +
  fear + beatings); the doctrine biases the scores (Chained doctrine + pain flips a
  Guardian-leaning history to Broken); a trickle of treatment stays Unevolved; beast status
  matches config per path; the system adopts Guardian, **event-logs the from→to transition
  and locks** it; a locked path is permanent under contrary treatment; and the beast stays
  band-exempt and absent from the sanity records on every path.
- PlayMode: `Abbey.Tests.PlayMode.HoundEvolutionPlayModeTests` — **3 tests**. Across
  simulated days: feeding + co-fighting hardens the hound into Guardian and it then defends
  at night; a neglected, starving hound hunting alone hardens into Starved and **refuses the
  bell** (reusing the P2-05 starved assertion for continuity); and on every path the beast is
  exempt from the Dark-band penalties and never enters the sanity records.

## Visual verification

1. `Tools → Abbey → Build Prototype Scene`, then press Play. `WorldSystems` now also holds a
   `HoundEvolutionSystem`; the chained Black Hound sits at the tower base as before.
2. Press **F1** (DebugOverlay) and find the **Black Hound** section. Below the bond values
   you now see a `treatment:` line (feed / allied / solo / rites / chain / injuries), then
   `PATH=… doctrine=… beastStatus=…` and a `scores` row with the five path values.
3. Drive treatment across fast-forwarded days and watch the path form:
   - **Guardian** — feed the hound and let it fight beside the hero (the existing feed/bell
     debug actions), keep it fed. **Correct:** the `Guard` score climbs, at the configured
     threshold `PATH` flips to `Guardian` (then `LOCKED`), `hound_evolved` appears in the
     log, `beastStatus` goes positive, and the hound answers the bell and engages raiders
     near the villagers.
   - **Starved** — never feed it; free it and let it hunt monsters alone. **Correct:** the
     `Starv` score climbs, `PATH` flips to `Starved`, `beastStatus` goes negative, the hound
     drags a kill into the dark and **ignores the bell** (`hound_ignored_bell path=Starved`
     in the log), extending the P2-05 starved branch into a permanent path.
   - **War / Sacred / Broken** — constant Hound-Hunt combat (War, feared but strong),
     candle/rite offerings under a Sacred doctrine (Sacred, calm, fights only at the abbey),
     or chaining + beating under a Chained doctrine (Broken, unreliable) shift the matching
     scores and flip the label the same way.
   **Failure:** the path label never flips despite lopsided treatment, a path score does not
   track its treatment counters, the transition is missing from the log, the beast picks up
   a sanity record or a Dark-band damage/sanity debuff on any path, or a locked path changes.
4. Cross-check balance against data, not code: open `HoundEvolutionConfig` (or the coded
   defaults in `Runtime/Beast/HoundEvolutionConfig.cs`) and confirm the per-path weights,
   `pathAdoptThreshold` / `pathLockThreshold`, behaviour parameter sets and beast-status
   values match what the F1 panel shows.
