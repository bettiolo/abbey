# P3-08 — Emergency overdrive actions (Candle Line et al.)

## What changed

The player's **panic buttons**: seven one-night emergency levers that solve tonight at a
cost. A new **`OverdriveSystem`** (on `WorldSystems`, singleton, `[ExecuteAlways]`) fires a
lever, pays its **immediate** costs — resources debited from the `ResourceLedger` (candles,
oil), a sharp sanity hit on each participating villager, a signed trust / beast-status delta
into the P3-10 stub ledgers (`TrustLedger` / `BeastStatusLedger` in `Runtime/Core`) — and
books its **deferred** costs: a dread the `SanitySystem` adds to each participant at dawn,
and **nightmare-debt points** the `NightmareDirector` cashes in on later nights (extra
monsters on top of the season wave). The levers are:

- **Forced Night Work** — a job site works the night: big sanity + trust cost, heavy debt.
- **Candle Line** — N villagers become candle carriers holding **mobile `LightSource`s**
  that light a route/worksite Safe; candles up front + candles per hour; the core visual.
- **Lantern Overburn** — chosen lanterns burn brighter (radius ×) at a multiplied fuel rate.
- **Bell Toll** — rings a recall/hesitation bell pulse; trust erodes if leaned on.
- **Abbey Rite** — burns candles for a calm aura; books the heaviest old-faith / nightmare debt.
- **Hound Hunt** — spends beast standing; one-night cooldown.
- **Volunteer Watch** — trusted villagers stand watch at a sanity cost.

Per-night lifecycle: `Activate` (immediate costs + drive the effect + exempt participants
from the dusk recall) → `Tick` (per-hour upkeep; a lever out of candles/oil stands down with
a risk tag) → `SettleAtDawn` (hand villagers back, restore lights, add deferred dread +
pool the nightmare debt). Every activation and cost is **event-logged**; deterministic (no
RNG). All balance is data in **`OverdriveConfig`** (Resources path `OverdriveConfig`, coded
defaults — no asset required). P3-09 laws gate levers via `OverdriveSystem.PermissionProvider`
/ `IsPermitted` (default: everything allowed); P3-11 nightmares consume
`PendingNightmareDebt` via `ConsumeNightmareDebtForNight()`.

Modified: `LightSource` (overburn mode), `VillagerAgent` (`NightWorkExempt` recall exemption),
`NightmareDirector` (debt → extra monsters), `Editor/PrototypeSceneBuilder` (registers the
system + the F12 debug panel). Debug surface: **`OverdriveDebugPanel`** (F12).

## Visual verification

1. `Tools → Abbey → Build Prototype Scene`, then press Play. `WorldSystems` now also holds an
   `OverdriveSystem`; `DebugPanels` holds an `OverdriveDebugPanel`.
2. Press **F12** to open the **Overdrive** panel (right side). It lists the seven levers with
   a `[poor]` / `[barred]` flag, the levers active tonight (participants / candle carriers /
   overburned lanterns), and the `pending debt` + `tonight +` nightmare-debt readouts. The
   number keys **1-7** fire the matching lever while the panel is open.
3. At **dusk**, fire **Candle Line** (key `2`). **Correct:** a chain of small light gizmos
   appears along the road out from camp; the road classifies Safe (villagers assigned there
   keep working through the night instead of recalling); open **EconomyDebugPanel** (F2) and
   watch the **candle** count fall each in-game hour. **Broken:** no carrier lights appear,
   the road stays Dark, workers recall at dusk anyway, or the candle count never drops.
4. Fire **Lantern Overburn** (key `3`). **Correct:** the chosen lanterns' light gizmos grow
   and their fuel bars fall faster (an `overdrive_risk out_of_oil` log + a guttered lantern if
   they empty mid-night). At **dawn** the levers stand down — carriers vanish, lanterns return
   to normal radius, workers are handed back to the recall — and the F12 panel's `pending
   debt` becomes nonzero.
5. Fire a **debt-heavy** lever (Abbey Rite, key `5`) one night, then let the day cycle. **On a
   later night** the `NightmareDirector` spawns extra monsters — the F12 `pending debt` drops
   and a `night_escalation debt_monsters` record appears in the log; the night is visibly
   rougher than a no-debt run under the same seed. **Broken:** the pending-debt readout stays
   zero after firing a lever, or later nights spawn the same count as the no-debt baseline.
6. Cross-check balance against data, not code: open `OverdriveConfig` (or the coded defaults
   in `Runtime/Decrees/OverdriveConfig.cs`) and confirm each lever's resource / sanity / trust
   / beast-status costs, nightmare-debt points, cooldown and effect parameters, plus the
   `debtToMonsterFactor` / `maxDebtMonstersPerNight` / `debtDrainFraction` conversion, match
   what the F12 panel and the logs show.
